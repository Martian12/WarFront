Recommended Books

The Guns of August by Barbara W. Tuchman

For anyone who want to understand more about the first few months of World War I, The Guns of August is a must-read. The book first discusses the events leading up to the conflict before giving a thorough account of the British-French effort to prevent the German army from reaching Paris. The book is a great resource for learning more about the horrors of trench warfare and how they came to dominate the conflict.

Documentary

They Shall Not Grow Old

The 2018 documentary They Shall Not Grow Old was made and directed by Peter Jackson. The video was made utilising authentic First World War footage from the Imperial War Museum's archives, the majority of which had never been seen before and was, at the time of its publication, more than a century old. The audio comes from Imperial War Museum (IWM) and BBC interviews with British service members who participated in the war. The majority of the footage has been coloured and altered using contemporary production methods, and voice acting and sound effects have been added to make it more evocative and feel more like the troops' actual experiences.
