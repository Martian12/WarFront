Weapons of the Russo-Ukrainian War

In early 2014, the Euromaidan protests led to the ousting of Ukraine's pro-Russian president, Viktor Yanukovych, and sparked ongoing conflicts with Russia. In 2022, Russia invaded Ukraine, leading to international condemnation and a devastating war with thousands of casualties.
